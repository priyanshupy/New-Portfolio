Frequently Asked Questions

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our AI analyzes lifestyle signals like sleep patterns, cleanliness preferences, cooking habits, guest policies, pet ownership, and work-from-home arrangements to calculate compatibility scores.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize user privacy and implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All information is encrypted and never shared without consent.

What if I don't find a good match?

We continuously improve our algorithm and expand our user base. You can also adjust your preferences or wait for new users to join the platform.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption and never share your personal information with third parties. Our AI algorithms work with anonymized data to ensure your identity remains protected while finding the perfect roommate match.
